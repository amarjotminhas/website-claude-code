How we work

A process you can hold us to

  1. Consult

    We walk the site, listen to what you're after, and give you a straight answer on feasibility, budget range, and timeline.

  2. Design & price

    You get a fixed, line-item proposal — not an estimate that grows. We flag allowances and unknowns before you sign.

  3. Build

    A dedicated site supervisor, a weekly progress report with photos, and a jobsite kept clean enough to visit any day.

  4. Handover

    A full punch-list walkthrough, every manual and warranty in one binder, and a five-year workmanship guarantee.
