Services

Six trades. One accountable contractor.

Whatever the project, you deal with one estimator, one supervisor, and one warranty. Here's what we do — and exactly what's included.

01 · Residential

Custom Home Building

Your architect's plans or a design we develop together — either way, we build it once and build it right. Every Ironwood home is framed by our own crew, not the lowest bidder of the week.

  • Fixed-price contracts with line-item transparency
  • In-house framing and finish carpentry crews
  • Weekly photo reports and open-jobsite policy
  • Five-year workmanship warranty, transferable on sale
Quote a custom home

02 · Commercial

Commercial Construction

Offices, retail, restaurants, and light industrial. We know that every week a space sits unfinished costs you revenue, so we build the schedule backwards from your opening day.

  • Tenant improvements and ground-up construction
  • Permit management and city liaison handled for you
  • Phased occupancy scheduling to open sooner
  • After-hours work available for occupied buildings
Quote a commercial project

03 · Remodel

Renovations & Additions

Remodeling means working inside a home your family still lives in. Our crews dust-wall every work zone, sweep out daily, and keep one bathroom and your kitchen running as long as physically possible.

  • Kitchens, bathrooms, additions, and whole-home remodels
  • Structural work engineered and permitted in-house
  • Sealed containment and daily site cleanup
  • Fixed completion date written into the contract
Quote a renovation

04 · Design-build

Design-Build

One contract covers design and construction, so the person drawing your plans is accountable to the same budget as the person building them. No finger-pointing between architect and builder — they sit at the same table.

  • Concept-to-keys under a single agreement
  • Real construction pricing at every design milestone
  • Partner architects and engineers we've vetted for years
  • Value engineering before, not after, the budget breaks
Start a design-build conversation

05 · Structural

Concrete & Foundations

Everything we build stands on this trade, so we never sub it out. Footings, stem walls, slabs, retaining walls, and flatwork — poured by the same Ironwood crew that's done it for twenty years.

  • Foundations, slabs, driveways, and retaining walls
  • Seismic retrofits for older Portland homes
  • Engineered mixes specified per soil report
  • Third-party testing on every structural pour

06 · Oversight

Project Management

Already have a builder or handling an owner-build? Hire our management team alone. We run the schedule, the budget, and the subcontractors while you keep control of the checkbook.

  • Owner's-representative and construction management
  • Bid leveling and subcontractor qualification
  • Cost tracking with monthly variance reports
  • Quality inspections at every trade handoff

Every service, same discipline

The Ironwood process

  1. Consult

    We walk the site, listen to what you're after, and give you a straight answer on feasibility, budget range, and timeline.

  2. Design & price

    You get a fixed, line-item proposal — not an estimate that grows. We flag allowances and unknowns before you sign.

  3. Build

    A dedicated site supervisor, a weekly progress report with photos, and a jobsite kept clean enough to visit any day.

  4. Handover

    A full punch-list walkthrough, every manual and warranty in one binder, and a five-year workmanship guarantee.
